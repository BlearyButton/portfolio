Momoyoga
SUMMARY
Momoyoga is a simple and complete management software, specially made for yogateachers and yoga studio's. Currently, the yoga studios have their own personal website, and they integrate the software onto their website. Adding a 'one stop shop' would mean they no longer need to use multiple platforms, and the information that is now on different websites, all can be placed onto Momoyoga's software. Meaning, the yoga teachers can customize the software to fit the styling of their studio.
What are the possibilities for a one stop shop within Momoyoga, looking at the development of the market, and the needs of the current users?

The company
Momoyoga is a SAAS company. This means they offer software as a service to their customers. Momoyoga is made specially for yoga studios and yoga teachers and is made with the needs and requirements of the yoga studios and teacher in the back of their head.
Momoyoga originates from the problem that a lot of yoga teachers and yoga studios are losing time on affairs such as payments and keeping their agenda up to date. They could have spent this time teaching their yoga lessons. So, this is a waste of time, where Momoyoga comes in to play. The software is developed with this problem in mind.
On the current market, there isn’t a software that is fully focused on yoga. Most software’s are made for all different kinds of sports, Momoyoga focuses fully on yoga. This is a conscious decision, to specifically cater to the needs of the yoga teachers.

Results
The possibilities for a one stop shop are the most important possibilities that are in line with the needs of the current users of Momoyoga, are adding the About page and the Contact page. The target group of Momoyoga likes simplicity. They specifically choose Momoyoga because this is an easy to use software without too many options they would never use anyway. Because of this, it is key to keep the options for the one stop shop as simple as possible. With adding these customizable features, Momoyoga can be seen as a better option for most studios in comparison to plugins / apps that are currently becoming more and more popular. These plugins / apps have lots of features, in which customization features are the biggest reasons as to why studios choose them over Momoyoga. But, adding and keeping these plugins up to date takes up a lot of time. With this information, adding the About page and Contact page to the Momoyoga software is very nice as long as these features are simple and easy to use.
