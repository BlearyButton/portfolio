Hi there! Nice to meet you, I am Stephanie. I am a Dutch visual designer with a great passion for UI & UX design. Currently, I am studying ICT & Media Design and will be graduating in July 2023.
During my studies 📚, I have always played with the thought to one day start my own business. I would always get inspired when checking out others who have done the same, and in 2021 finally decided to go for it.
Since starting my business, I have been blessed to have worked with some great companies. Each project has been completely different than the other, meaning one week I will work on designing a complete mobile application by creating user interfaces, and the other week I will create prints for a business' stationery.
I design for the magic ✨ of a user-first experience, combined with clean and minimalistic design. As both my interests, and my qualities lie on all different kinds of design work, feel free to contact me about any sort of project you have, and we can discuss how I may be at your service!
